# Privacy & Terms

## Privacy Policy

Device activation IDs and license keys are sent to the Sutto license server to verify subscription status and manage device activations.

### Third-Party Services

- **Payment provider**: Subscription payments are processed by a third-party payment service. Your payment information is handled directly by the payment provider and is not stored by Sutto. Please refer to the payment provider's privacy policy for details.

## Terms of Service

### License Terms

- Sutto is offered as a **subscription-based** service
- Each subscription supports up to **3 device activations**
- Activating on a 4th device automatically deactivates the oldest device

### Trial Terms

- New users receive a **30-day free trial** with full access to all features
- Trial days are counted based on actual usage days, not calendar days
- After the trial expires, a subscription is required to continue using the extension
